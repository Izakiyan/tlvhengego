TLVhenge? Manhattanhenge?
What is it all about?

Manhattanhenge — sometimes referred to as the Manhattan Solstice, is an event during which the setting sun is aligned with the east–west streets of the main street grid of Manhattan, New York City.

Tel Aviv grid system is asymmetric thus, the event occurs several times during the year.
Use #TLVhenge in instagram to help us find the best street so we can make an official Henge day in Tel Aviv!
